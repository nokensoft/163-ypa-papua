About Detail

Our story and organizational direction

YPA Papua has grown from local initiative into a trusted partner for education, mission, and social development.

Background

Founded by young Papuans, the organization continues to empower people through contextual and practical development programs.

Growth Path

From early outreach initiatives to structured education and intercultural service, the foundation expanded its impact over time.

Current Role

Today, YPA Papua supports leadership preparation, literacy, social entrepreneurship, and collaborative community work.

Service timeline

1990s

Initial foundation and youth-focused preparation efforts.

2000s

Program expansion in education and mission activities.

2010s

Institutional strengthening and wider collaboration.

2020s

Integrated impact through multi-sector community programs.
